Discover, track, and stream anime in one premium experience.

FeatherNeko brings anime discovery, watchlists, episode streaming, discussions, reviews, and airing schedules into one fast, cinematic mobile experience.

Open App Explore Features
● Fast local caching ● Anime discovery ● Watchlist + progress ● Rich discussions ● Airing schedules ● Streaming-ready playback

Discover anime without the clutter

Browse trending shelves, explore by genre, and search with precision. The interface is built for scanning and discovery—no visual noise, no endless scrolling through dense grids. Find what you want, quickly.

Stay on top of what you're watching

Your watchlist, continue watching, and progress stay in sync. Pick up where you left off. See what's airing this week. FeatherNeko keeps you aware without feeling like a chore.

Reviews, discussions, and anime context—built in

Rich discussion sheets, reviews, and detail tabs that feel native on mobile. BBCode, HTML, and mixed content are rendered cleanly. No janky webviews—just readable, well-formatted context where you need it.

Built to feel fast, even when the data isn't simple

Multi-source anime, mixed content formats, and heavy metadata. FeatherNeko handles it with caching, request efficiency, and resilient loading.

Smart caching

Multi-layer caching with source-aware freshness and stale-while-revalidate behavior.

Request efficiency

Duplicate requests are collapsed and cache effectiveness is surfaced in-app.

Rich content rendering

BBCode, HTML, and mixed provider content are normalized into one rendering pipeline.

Premium playback UX

Custom controls and thoughtful loading states keep streaming polished.

Designed like a product, not a prototype

Designed and engineered by Karuifeather

FeatherNeko is a frontend consumer app that brings together the best of different anime APIs—AniList, MyAnimeList, and Kitsu—into one premium mobile experience. Multi-layer caching, normalized content rendering, and thoughtful UX make it fast and clear.
